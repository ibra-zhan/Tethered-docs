Welcome to Tethered

Tethered is a mobile application designed to help families stay connected through daily photo check-ins, mood tracking, and shared journaling.

Features

  • Daily Check-ins: Share photos and moments with your family every day
  • Streak Tracking: Build consistent habits with visual streak counters
  • Family Timeline: Private, family-only sharing of your journey
  • Secure Connections: Connect with family members via invite codes
